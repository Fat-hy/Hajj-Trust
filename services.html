Hajj Package Details

Hajj is one of the Five Pillars of Islam and a mandatory act of worship for every Muslim who is physically and financially able to undertake the journey. It involves a series of rituals performed in and around the holy city of Mecca in Saudi Arabia, culminating in a profound spiritual experience.

Our Hajj package is meticulously designed to offer pilgrims a comprehensive and spiritually enriching experience. This package includes:

  • Accommodation in comfortable hotels close to the Holy Sites
  • Transportation to and from the pilgrimage locations
  • Meals throughout the journey
  • Guided tours and assistance during the pilgrimage
  • Ihram clothing and travel bags
  • Pre-Hajj orientation sessions to prepare you for the pilgrimage
  • 24/7 support from our experienced team to ensure your journey is smooth and hassle-free

The Hajj pilgrimage is a once-in-a-lifetime spiritual journey for Muslims. Fulfilling this duty is a profound expression of faith and devotion. Our package ensures that every aspect of your trip is carefully planned and managed, allowing you to focus on the spiritual significance of the pilgrimage.

With over 20 years of experience, Tanzania Muslim Hajj Trust (TMHT) has established itself as the best option for Hajj services in Tanzania, offering unmatched expertise and dedication to your spiritual journey.

Hajj by Proxy Details

Hajj by Proxy is a service that allows individuals who are unable to travel due to health, age, or other constraints to fulfill their religious obligation. This service ensures that the rites of Hajj are performed on their behalf by a trusted representative.

Our Hajj by Proxy service is specifically designed for individuals who are unable to travel but wish to fulfill their religious obligation. This service includes:

  • All necessary arrangements for the Hajj rites to be performed on your behalf
  • Selection of a trusted and experienced proxy to carry out the pilgrimage
  • Regular updates on the progress of the pilgrimage to keep you informed
  • Confirmation of the completion of Hajj rites once performed

For those unable to physically perform Hajj due to health, age, or other constraints, Hajj by Proxy offers a way to fulfill this important religious duty. This service ensures that you can meet your spiritual obligations even if you cannot travel.

Common use cases for Hajj by Proxy include individuals with chronic illnesses, elderly persons, and those unable to travel due to personal constraints. TMHT provides a reliable and transparent Hajj by Proxy service, ensuring all rites are performed with utmost sincerity and adherence to religious requirements.

With over 20 years of expertise, Tanzania Muslim Hajj Trust remains the best choice for ensuring your spiritual obligations are met with care and professionalism.

Step-by-Step Hajj Booking

Follow these simple steps to secure your pilgrimage package with Tanzania Muslim Hajj Trust.

Step 1: Explore, Select, and Review Packages

Visit our website and navigate to the "Our Services" section to explore, select, and review the available packages. Each package is detailed with information regarding the itinerary, inclusions, and pricing.

Step 2: Visit Our Office to Confirm Availability

Visit our office to finalize your booking. Our dedicated team will assist you in selecting the right package and completing the necessary paperwork. They will also confirm the availability of the selected package for your chosen dates.

Step 3: Proceed with Payment

After confirming availability, proceed with the payment process. You can make payments through our banking systems, and transactions can be made in both TZS (Tanzanian Shillings) and USD (United States Dollars).

Step 4: Payment Confirmation

Once payment is completed, you will receive confirmation of your booking along with a receipt. This confirms your secured spot for the pilgrimage.

Step 5: Pre-Travel Seminar

Attend our informative pre-travel seminar held before the departure date. Gain insights into the pilgrimage process, required documentation, and other essential details to make your journey seamless.

Step 6: Post-Seminar Support

After the seminar, we provide continued support, including updates related to the trip, travel arrangements, accommodations, and more to ensure you're well-prepared.

Step 7: Travel Documentation Check

Ensure you have all the required travel documents, including your passport, visa, and any additional documentation specified by our team before your departure.

Step 8: Departure

Embark on your journey for Hajj with confidence, knowing that Tanzania Muslim Hajj Trust has handled all the necessary arrangements for your pilgrimage.
